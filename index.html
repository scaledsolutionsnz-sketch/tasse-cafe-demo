Our story

A little corner of Dunedin built around good coffee.

Tasse is the kind of cafe you end up coming back to without really planning it — somewhere the coffee is consistent, the welcome is real, and the room invites you to slow down for a minute.

We keep the list tight and do it well: dial the espresso in every morning, bake through the day, and look after regulars and first-timers exactly the same. No fuss, just a good cup and a good seat.

  • Locally run, in the heart of Dunedin
  • Espresso dialled in fresh every day
  • Baking made in-house, dietaries looked after
