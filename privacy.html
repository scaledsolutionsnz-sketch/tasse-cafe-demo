1. The short version

We only collect what we need to reply to you, and we don't sell your information. This sample policy should be reviewed before the site goes live.

2. What we collect

When you use a form on this site we collect the details you enter — typically your name, email, phone number and your message or feedback. We don't ask for payment details through this website.

3. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry, booking or catering request
  • To act on feedback and improve the cafe
  • To keep a record of our conversation with you

4. How forms are handled

Website forms are delivered to us by email through a third-party form service (FormSubmit). Your message is transmitted to our inbox; please avoid sending sensitive personal information through the form.

5. Sharing

We don't sell or rent your information. We only share it where needed to run the cafe (for example, our email provider) or where required by law.

6. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Get in touch and we'll help.

7. Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies, mainly for basic functionality. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email tassecafe23@gmail.com or call 027 525 7770.
